ABOUT

Hayas and Associates was established in 2006. The firm offers Architectural, Landscape, Project Management and Interior design consultancy services for projects ranging from Individual Bungalows, Residential, Educational, Apartments, Resorts, Restaurants, Commercial complexes, Marriage Halls, Container Offices etc., We seek to achieve our goals by creating an office envionment that facilitates creativity, team work, leadership, quality, consciousness and continual improvement through innovation. We have designed many projects in and around the world. We have gained name by producing unique designs using state of the art software, 3D modeling and computer aided drawings.. We work in collaboration with other consultants like structural consultants, Electrical consultants, plumbing consultants, etc.
